The reality before Rosetta

Amazon fulfilment is not designed to be simple

For most brands on Vendor Central, fulfilment is an ongoing source of cost, chaos, and margin erosion. These are the problems we eliminate from day one.

01

Up to 31 fulfilment centres to manage

Standard Amazon vendor arrangements route your products across the UK's fulfilment network. That means up to 31 separate shipments, 31 sets of labels, and 31 different delivery slots to coordinate every single week.

02

Intermittent, unpredictable purchase orders

Orders can arrive at any point in the week, in any volume. Your operations team cannot plan. Production schedules slip. Despatch becomes reactive. The constant firefighting compounds over time into a significant overhead.

03

Chargebacks and prep charges eating margin

Late delivery windows, labelling non-compliance, packaging shortfalls. Amazon issues chargebacks for every infraction and the deductions compound quietly into serious margin erosion. Many brands don't even know how much they're losing.

How it works

Five steps. One simple cycle. Every week.

Our platform consolidates Amazon's complex fulfilment requirements into a single predictable weekly process. The whole thing runs on automation so your team only needs to do one thing: ship.

Fulfilment rate matters. Our model is designed to keep your fulfilment rate consistently above 80%. This directly reduces your Partner Success Fee from 8% to 5%. Good logistics is good commercial practice.

Shipping labels and PO confirmation generated

Our platform integrates directly with Amazon Vendor Central via API. The moment the PO is confirmed, your ready-to-print labels are generated. No manual process, no data entry, no admin burden on your team.

One destination. One set of paperwork. One collection booking. We consolidate all product lines into a single inbound shipment routed to the most appropriate FC for your portfolio. Your warehouse team gets a clean, simple job.
