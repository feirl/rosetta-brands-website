The problem today

Your Amazon data is everywhere — and visible nowhere

Most FMCG vendors piece together their Amazon performance picture from weekly emails, spreadsheet exports, and fragmented dashboards. By the time you see the data, it is already out of date.

01

Visibility requires asking someone

Want to know how last week's orders performed? What the current fill rate is? Whether a new ASIN has a healthy Buy Box? Right now, the answer involves an email to your account manager and a wait. A portal should give you that instantly — without the need to ask.

02

Data lives in disconnected places

Sales in one spreadsheet. Orders in a portal you can barely navigate. Advertising performance somewhere else. Fulfilment status in yet another system. Stitching it together into a coherent picture is manual work that no commercial team should have to do every week.

03

Collaboration happens in email

Requests for imagery, approvals for new listings, outstanding documents, open questions about strategy — all of it managed through email chains that lose context, miss stakeholders, and leave no audit trail. There is no shared space for your team and ours to work together properly.

What we are building

A single environment for your entire Amazon operation

The Client Portal is designed to be the central hub for everything that happens between your brand and Amazon — bringing live data, operational workflows, documents, and strategic reporting into one intuitive platform.

It is not a static report or a read-only dashboard. It is a working environment where your team and ours collaborate in real time, with full visibility in both directions.

Built on live API data. Every data point in the portal connects directly to the Amazon Vendor ecosystem and Rosetta's own platforms — updating in real time, not on a weekly export cycle.
Access

Secure login via OTP — no passwords to manage

The portal uses one-time passcode authentication, removing the security risk of shared passwords and the friction of password resets. Your team gets access at the role level they need — full visibility for commercial leads, operational views for logistics teams — with a login that takes seconds.

OTP authentication Role-based access Zero password friction
Performance

Live sales, advertising, and inventory data in one view

Your dashboard surfaces revenue performance, order velocity, advertising metrics, and inventory levels — updated continuously from the Amazon Vendor ecosystem. See what is happening right now, not what happened last Tuesday. Drill into individual ASINs, time periods, or product categories without waiting for a custom report.

Live revenue data ASIN-level reporting Inventory visibility
Operations

Purchase orders, shipments, and fulfilment — tracked end to end

Every purchase order from Amazon is visible in the portal: its current status, documentation, shipping progress, and confirmation timeline. Orders that are processing automatically show their status. Those that need review are flagged clearly. Your operations team has a live view of every shipment — from booking to Amazon receipt — without touching Vendor Central.

PO tracking Shipment visibility Fulfilment status
Documents & Requests

Imagery, documentation, and key requests — one managed workspace

Upload imagery for new and existing ASINs, manage listing assets, track open requests, and access all your account documentation in one place. Requests have clear ownership, status, and history — visible to both your team and ours — replacing the email chains and shared drives that currently make collaboration slow and opaque.

Imagery management Request tracking Document access
Market Intelligence

Category trends and external signals alongside your own data

The portal surfaces market-level insights alongside your account data — category trends, competitive signals, and external market intelligence that inform forecasting and strategy. Combined with Rosetta's proprietary forecasting models and our direct relationships inside Amazon, this gives your team a wider commercial context that most vendors simply don't have access to.

Category insights Market trends Rosetta forecasting
The difference it makes

From asking for data to owning it

The portal shifts the nature of the relationship between Rosetta and your team. Rather than being dependent on weekly reports and account manager availability for visibility, you have live access to everything that matters — on demand, at any hour.

This changes how you plan, how you respond to Amazon signals, and how quickly you can act on opportunities. Data that previously arrived in a Tuesday email becomes something you can act on Monday morning.

Common Questions

Common questions about the Client Portal

Specific questions about this part of the Platform. For broader questions about how Rosetta Brands works, visit our FAQ page.

The Client Portal is in active development and will be available to all Rosetta Brands partner brands. If you are joining as a new client, access to the portal will be part of your onboarding. Existing clients will be migrated in phases, with full support from our team at each stage. Contact us to find out where you are in the rollout schedule.

The portal replaces the fragmented mix of email threads, spreadsheet reports, and disconnected dashboards that most FMCG brands currently rely on for visibility into their Amazon Vendor performance. It brings orders, fulfilment status, performance reporting, document management, and communications into a single environment — less context-switching, fewer missed updates, and a single source of truth for your entire Amazon relationship with Rosetta.

The portal is designed to be intuitive and does not require technical knowledge to use. The interface is built around the tasks clients actually need to complete — reviewing orders, checking performance, downloading documents, and raising requests. We provide a guided onboarding walkthrough for all new users, and our team is available to support you throughout. The goal is to make visibility effortless, not to add complexity.

The portal connects directly to the Amazon Vendor ecosystem via secure API, pulling live data across orders, inventory, fulfilment, and retail analytics. It also draws on data from Rosetta's own operational platforms — including order automation and listing health monitoring — to give you a unified view. There is no manual data export or import. Everything updates in real time as activity happens in your Amazon account.

The portal gives you genuine self-service visibility that most FMCG vendors currently lack. Today, getting answers about orders, performance, or availability typically requires asking your account manager and waiting for a report. With the portal, you can see exactly what is happening in your Amazon account at any moment — order status, ASIN performance, fulfilment progress, and market context — without needing to raise a request. You can also upload imagery and documentation, track requests, and review forecasts directly in the platform.
