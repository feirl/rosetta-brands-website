The Problem

Amazon is complex. Most brands are going it alone.

The UK FMCG brands growing fastest on Amazon aren't doing it with a standard agency. They've changed the model entirely.

Locked out of vendor status

Amazon vendor accounts are rare, established, and structurally hard to obtain. Most FMCG brands are stuck on Seller Central, missing the Prime badge, EDI ordering, and the commercial leverage that comes with vendor relationships.

Chargebacks and compliance costs

Amazon's compliance rules are strict and constantly changing. Chargebacks, shortages, and listing suppressions eat into margins. Without dedicated compliance expertise and direct ABS access, brands spend more time firefighting than growing.

Misaligned agency incentives

Most Amazon agencies charge a retainer whether you grow or not. Their success and your success aren't the same thing. You want a partner whose revenue goes up only when yours does - not one billing you while results plateau.

How It Works

Everything included. One partnership.

VaaS isn't a service layer on top of your Amazon account. It replaces it. Your products are bought and sold through our vendor relationship.

01 - Vendor Access & Prime

Enterprise vendor infrastructure from day one

Rosetta Brands holds an established Amazon vendor account - the kind brands can't easily get on their own. When you partner with us, your products are listed and sold through our vendor relationship, giving you instant access to the full suite of vendor benefits.

  • Prime badge on every product from day one
  • EDI-based purchase ordering every Monday
  • Case-pack fulfilment only - no individual pick and pack
  • Zero chargebacks, returns, or storage costs to you
