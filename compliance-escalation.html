The Problem

Amazon's compliance machine runs against you.

Every vendor relationship with Amazon comes with a compliance framework designed for Amazon's benefit. Most brands discover the true cost when the invoices arrive.

15 types of chargeback, all automatic

Amazon operates 15 distinct chargeback categories. Penalties range from 2% to 9% of COGS per shipment, £425 per delivery incident, and up to €5 per unit for packaging failures. They are raised automatically against the vendor - without prior notice.

Up to 9% of COGS per shipment

Shortage claims can run to six figures

When Amazon's warehouse logs fewer units than arrived, the difference becomes a shortage claim - often caused by Amazon's own receiving errors. A single case can reach £898K. The dispute window is now just 18 months, and resolution takes 35+ days of active case management.

18-month dispute window (from April 2025)

FMCG regulatory requirements never stop changing

HFSS advertising restrictions, Natasha's Law allergen labelling, GPSR product safety rules, and alcohol age verification requirements create a compliance matrix unique to grocery and FMCG brands - and it shifts constantly with minimal notice.

HFSS Sponsored Ads ban - live from Jan 2026
How We Handle It

Built into the structure. Not bolted on.

Because Rosetta Brands is the vendor of record, compliance protection isn't a service layer - it's a structural consequence of how the model works.

01 - Chargeback Absorption

We take the charge. You keep your margin.

Amazon chargebacks are levied against the vendor of record. That vendor is Rosetta Brands - not your brand. When Amazon raises a chargeback, it arrives in our account. We dispute where possible, absorb where necessary, and you never see the deduction. This is only possible because of the Vendor-As-A-Service model structure.

  • All 15 chargeback types handled - zero client exposure
  • ASN, PO, delivery, packaging and labelling chargebacks absorbed
  • Penalties never deducted from your purchase order payments
  • Operational improvements driven by Rosetta Brands to reduce future charges

02 - Shortage Claim Management

Shortage disputes run for weeks. We run them for you.

Shortage claims require meticulous evidence: proof of delivery, bill of lading, carrier data, and often direct escalation through Amazon's SVS and Vendor Central case system. From the moment a claim is raised, Rosetta Brands initiates the dispute process - building the case, submitting evidence packs, and tracking resolution through to credit.

  • Immediate dispute initiation - evidence submitted within 48 hours
  • Full carrier and warehouse documentation tracked from despatch
  • Direct escalation via AVS Brand Specialist where required
  • 18-month dispute window actively managed - no claims left to expire

03 - Amazon Vendor Service (AVS)

Your dedicated Amazon Brand Specialist, included.

AVS is Amazon's premium vendor programme - invite-only, costing 2-7% of ContraCOGS, with a minimum 12-month commitment and no trial period. Rosetta Brands includes AVS access for qualifying partners. Your Brand Specialist manages catalogue integrity, content suppression, and availability escalations directly with Amazon's internal teams.

  • Dedicated Brand Specialist with direct Amazon access
  • Catalogue management: ASIN creation, variation setup, suppression fixes
  • Availability and out-of-stock escalations resolved faster
  • Shortage escalations via internal Amazon channel - not just Vendor Central

04 - FMCG Regulatory Compliance

The regulatory matrix, managed as standard.

UK FMCG brands on Amazon face a regulatory layer that non-grocery categories never encounter. HFSS scoring determines advertising eligibility. Natasha's Law mandates specific allergen display. GPSR requires documented safety responsibilities. Alcohol listings require age-verification compliance. Rosetta Brands tracks and manages all of it, across all your active SKUs.

  • HFSS scoring monitored - Sponsored Ads eligibility tracked per SKU
  • Natasha's Law allergen labelling verified across all grocery listings
  • GPSR product safety documentation - Responsible Person confirmed
  • Alcohol Challenge 25+ age verification - full listing compliance
FMCG compliance areas managed
HFSS - High Fat, Sugar & Salt
Volume promotion ban from Oct 2025. Sponsored Ads restriction for products scoring 4+. In force January 2026.
Natasha's Law - Allergen Labelling
14 mandatory allergens displayed on pre-packed-for-direct-sale food. Listing content verified against physical label.
GPSR - General Product Safety Regulation
Effective December 2024. EU Responsible Person required for non-food consumer goods sold into European markets.
Alcohol - Challenge 25+ Age Verification
Amazon's age verification and listing compliance requirements for spirits, beer, and wine categories.
