What You're Currently Missing

Seller Central has a ceiling

You've built something real on Seller Central. But the model has limits, and most of the biggest levers for FMCG growth on Amazon sit on the other side of them.

The Prime credibility gap

On Seller Central you are competing for the Buy Box against other sellers, and sometimes your own products. Customers see marketplace complexity where they expect simplicity. Vendor removes that friction entirely: your products are sold by Amazon, carry Prime unconditionally, and sit in the catalogue with the credibility that FMCG brands need to convert consistently.

The fee structure is working against you

Referral fees, FBA storage, returns, prep costs, they compound against your margin in ways that are easy to underestimate and hard to reverse. The Rosetta Vendor model is structurally cleaner: you ship once, you get paid once, there are no surprise deductions, and Rosetta absorbs 100% of any chargebacks that arise on the Amazon side. The difference in blended margin is material for most FMCG brands.

The programmes you can't access yet

Subscribe & Save at scale, Amazon Business, exclusive promotional placements, international Vendor markets, these are largely Vendor territory. They are also where the biggest compounding growth levers for FMCG brands live. As a Seller, you are looking at them from the outside. Through Rosetta, they are activated as standard from day one.
