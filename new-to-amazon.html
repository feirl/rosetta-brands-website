What's Holding You Back

The worries are understandable.
The reality is different.

Most FMCG brands that aren't on Amazon yet have the same three concerns. Here is what they actually look like once you are inside the Rosetta model.

The worry

"We don't have the resource or expertise to manage Amazon."

The reality

You don't need any. Rosetta Brands provides the entire operational infrastructure — vendor account management, fulfilment, compliance, advertising, content, technology, and reporting. Your team focuses on your product. Rosetta handles everything Amazon, from day one.

The worry

"Amazon will cause problems with our existing retail relationships."

The reality

Channel conflict is a legitimate concern — and one Rosetta has navigated hundreds of times. We work with you on pricing strategy from the outset to ensure Amazon complements your retail relationships rather than threatening them. Amazon captures online demand that bricks-and-mortar simply cannot reach. Done properly, it adds a channel without disrupting the ones you already have.

The worry

"The fees and complexity will destroy our margin before we even get going."

The reality

The Rosetta model has no Amazon fees on your P&L. No referral fees, no FBA costs, no chargebacks, no variable deductions. You set a wholesale price, you ship to a single fulfilment centre, and you get paid in full at 45 days EOM on proof of delivery. We model the specific economics for your products before you commit to a single thing.

How It Works

From no Amazon presence to live and growing — in weeks

Rosetta has a defined process for launching FMCG brands on Amazon from scratch. Every step is managed. Nothing is left for you to figure out.

You have done this zero times. Rosetta has done it 958 times. That gap is why the Rosetta partnership exists — so you get the outcome without the learning curve.
Step 01

Free consultation and financial modelling

We start by understanding your brand, your product range, and your commercial goals. Then we model the Amazon opportunity for your specific products — showing you exactly what the economics look like on the Rosetta model, which programmes apply to your category, and what a realistic trading trajectory looks like. You get a clear picture before committing to anything.

Step 02

Vendor access from day one — no invite needed

Amazon does not invite new brands onto Vendor easily. The wait can be months, and many brands never receive an invitation. Through Rosetta's existing vendor infrastructure, your brand accesses Vendor immediately. The access barrier — the most common reason FMCG brands delay — is gone before you've even packed a single carton.

No Amazon invitation required Prime from week one 1P vendor relationship
Step 03

Your catalogue built to convert from launch

Rosetta's team builds your product listings, A+ Content, Brand Store, and key feature imagery — all optimised for Amazon's search algorithm and for customer conversion. Listings built for you A+ Content included Brand Store created
Step 04

Operations set up before your first order arrives

Order Automation, EDI compliance, shipping label generation, fulfilment to a single FC — all configured and tested before Amazon raises its first purchase order. When it does arrive, everything runs automatically. You ship. Rosetta handles the rest. There is no scramble, no manual process, and no compliance risk.

Live and growing — not just launched

Advertising campaigns go live. Subscribe & Save is activated. Amazon Business is enabled. International markets are assessed. And the Platform gives you a live view of every order, every ASIN's health, and your full performance data — from week one. This is not just a launch.
