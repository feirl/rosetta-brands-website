01, Vendor-as-a-Service

Everything Amazon needs. None of the cost, complexity, or risk.

We buy your products at an agreed trade price and sell them on Amazon at your recommended retail price. The trade margin in between funds your vendor access, Prime fulfilment, compliance management, and full operational infrastructure, so your brand gets enterprise-grade Amazon coverage with none of the overhead.

For most brands, replicating this independently would require a dedicated Amazon team, a compliance function, and direct vendor status that Amazon no longer freely grants. As a Rosetta Brands partner brand, you get all of it from day one.

02, Partner Success

Our incentives are fully aligned. We win when you win.

Success on Amazon can only be achieved through a genuine partnership. Our model means Rosetta Brands has a direct stake in your growth, we share in the positive outcomes when they happen, and we absorb the risk when things are harder.

The Partner Success component is only applied above an agreed minimum sales threshold, and it adjusts based on your order fulfilment rate. If you fulfil well, your fee is lower. That alignment runs through everything we do.

Common Questions

Common questions about our pricing model

Specific questions about this service. For broader questions about how Rosetta Brands works, visit our FAQ page.

Our model has three components. Vendor-As-A-Service: you sell your products to us at an agreed price and we list on Amazon at your RRP, the margin in between funds your vendor access, fulfilment, compliance, and the full operational infrastructure. Partner Success: a percentage fee applied only to revenue above your agreed monthly target, which adjusts based on your fulfilment rate. Growth Marketing: an optional add-on covering in-house advertising, Brand Store, A+ Content, and imagery for brands that want to accelerate further.

No upfront fees and no retainer. The Vendor-As-A-Service component is self-funded through the margin between your agreed sell-in price and your Amazon RRP - there is no separate invoice for it. The Partner Success component only applies above your agreed monthly sales target. If you don't grow beyond this target, the minimum success fee of £500pm applies. If the Partner Success fee exceeds the minimum success fee of £500, the Partner Success fee will apply in place of the minimum success fee, not in addition to.

The Partner Success component only applies to revenue above a mutually agreed monthly sales target. The target is set at onboarding based on your current Amazon revenue and realistic growth potential. Only revenue beyond that threshold triggers the Partner Success component.

We ask for an initial partnership term to allow the account infrastructure to bed in and performance to stabilise, typically six months. After that, the partnership continues on a rolling basis.

The growth target is agreed collaboratively at onboarding. We look at your current Amazon revenue run rate, category growth rates, and realistic timelines. The target should be achievable, an unachievable target is not in our interest either.
