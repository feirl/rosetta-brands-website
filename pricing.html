01 — Vendor-as-a-Service

Everything Amazon needs. None of the cost, complexity, or risk.

You sell your product to us at an agreed price. We list it on Amazon at your RRP. The margin in between covers your vendor access, fulfilment, compliance, and operational infrastructure — so your brand gets enterprise-grade Amazon coverage without the overhead.

The value packed into this component is substantial. Most brands trying to replicate it independently would need a dedicated Amazon team, a compliance operation, and direct vendor status — which Amazon no longer freely grants.

02 — Partner Success Fee

Our incentives are fully aligned. We win when you win.

Success on Amazon can only be achieved through a genuine partnership. Our model means Rosetta Brands has a direct stake in your growth — we share in the positive outcomes when they happen, and we absorb the risk when things are harder.

The Partner Success Fee is only applied above an agreed minimum sales threshold, and it adjusts based on your order fulfilment rate. If you fulfil well, your fee is lower. That alignment runs through everything we do.
