Common questions

Everything you need to know about working with Rosetta Brands. More on our full FAQ page.

Vendor-as-a-Service (VaaS) means Rosetta Brands acts as your Amazon vendor, we hold the vendor account relationship with Amazon, purchase your stock, and manage your entire presence on the platform. You get the revenue and the Prime badge without the operational complexity of running a vendor account yourself.

We work with consumer brands at every stage, from challenger brands wanting to launch on Amazon for the first time, to established consumer goods businesses looking to scale faster or recover margin. If your products belong on shelf, they belong on Amazon, and we can help you get there.

We currently operate across Amazon UK, Amazon Ireland, Amazon Germany, and Amazon Australia. Brands benefit from a single partnership that spans multiple markets, with localised pricing, fulfilment, and advertising managed centrally.

Onboarding typically takes 2–4 weeks from contract signature. We handle the account setup, catalogue build, and first purchase order, you supply the stock and we take it from there. For brands already on Amazon, migration timelines vary depending on your current setup.

A typical agency advises you and charges a management fee, you still own the operational headache. Rosetta Brands becomes your vendor: we buy your stock, take on the Amazon relationship, manage fulfilment, run advertising, and share in your commercial success. Our incentives are aligned with yours from day one.

We act as the operational and commercial engine behind your Amazon presence, but all major decisions, from pricing floors to new product launches, are made collaboratively. You have full visibility through our reporting platform.
