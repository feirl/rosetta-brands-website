Pricing

We only win
when you win.

No retainers, no hidden fees. Our pricing is fully aligned with your success - a transparent two-component model designed so we grow together.

See full pricing
Component 1

Partner Efficiencies

You sell to us at your agreed price. We sell to Amazon at the negotiated vendor price. The margin in between covers your vendor access, fulfilment management, and operational infrastructure.

Component 2

Partner Success Fee

A percentage fee on sales invoiced above your monthly target - we earn more only when you're growing. 5% at >80% fulfilment rate, 8% below. Minimum £500/brand/month.
