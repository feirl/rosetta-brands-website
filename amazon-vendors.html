Challenges We Solve

What's eating your Amazon margin

Existing vendor accounts face a different set of problems from sellers. These are the three we see most often. These are the challenges we can solve.

Complete Margin Erosion

From your Amazon fixed vendor terms to your unmanageable variable chargebacks, managing your margin soon becomes a nightmare. T&Cs negotiations, shortages, packaging and labelling, late shipments – the list is endless! These all trigger financial deductions that compound over months. Without dedicated compliance expertise and direct AVS access, these costs quietly destroy your profitability.

Compliance complexity and account risk

Listing suppressions, buy box losses, catalogue errors, and policy changes all require immediate, expert responses. Most brands don't have the internal bandwidth or direct Amazon relationships to handle these proactively. Small issues become expensive problems.

Underutilised programs and opportunities

Most vendor brands are unaware of or unable to access the full range of exclusive Amazon programs. Limited by their account ceiling but wanting to accelerate growth, they're cautious of what to explore and wondering if their operations and margin can support such programs. Without the confidence and support, you're stuck and sales become stagnant.

No fees. Paid on delivery.

Traditional vendor arrangements mean 90-day EOM payment terms, vendor fees, chargebacks, returns, overstock, damage, prep, and late fees. With Rosetta Brands, that entire cost structure disappears. You're paid in full on 45-day EOM on proof of delivery.

  • 100% of chargebacks absorbed by Rosetta Brands

Challenge 02 – Logistics & Operations

Simple. Predictable. Automated.

Case pack ordering issues, intermittent POs, and manual processing across up to 31 fulfilment centres creates constant operational chaos. We consolidate everything: weekly Monday POs, automated shipping labels in minutes, delivery to a single FC.

  • Weekly Monday PO – predictable, never intermittent
  • Automated shipping labels generated in minutes
  • Single FC delivery – not 31 separate shipments
  • EDI & labelling compliance fully managed
