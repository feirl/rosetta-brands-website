01 - Subscribe & Save

Recurring revenue, locked in. Ranking, lifted.

Subscribe & Save converts one-time buyers into a subscription base that reorders automatically - reducing acquisition cost, raising LTV, and signalling recurring demand to Amazon's ranking algorithm. The right discount tier isn't obvious: too low and you lose the ranking signal; too high and you erode margin unnecessarily. Rosetta Brands sets the tier strategically per product category.

  • Products enrolled at 10% discount see 1.8x average conversion uplift
  • Algorithm ranking boost documented even at 2% subscription rate - position jumps of 14+ places within 10 days
  • SnS customers buy more frequently and spend more per visit than one-time buyers
  • Vendor Powered Vouchers (VPVs) managed alongside SnS for first-purchase incentives

03 - Deals & Prime Day

The biggest traffic events on Amazon. Managed to deadline.

Prime Day is the largest sales event in Amazon's calendar. 300 million items sold over two days in 2024. $14.2 billion in total sales. Lightning Deals, Best Deals, Deal of the Day, and Prime Day promotions each have different eligibility rules, fee structures, and submission deadlines - and those rules change every year. Rosetta Brands tracks the calendar and manages the full submission process.

  • Lightning Deals submitted 4-6 weeks before each major event window
  • Eligibility criteria managed - 15% minimum discount, star rating, review thresholds
  • Best Deals negotiated for multi-SKU or catalogue-level promotions
  • Post-deal analysis - conversion rate, halo effect, net margin per deal type

Common Questions

Amazon programmes - answered

Questions specific to these programmes. For broader questions about how Vendor-As-A-Service works, visit our FAQ page.

Subscribe and Save lets customers set up recurring deliveries at a discount. As a vendor, you fund 0%, 5% or 10% of the discount - Amazon co-funds additional savings on the higher tiers. Products enrolled at 10% discount see an average 1.8x conversion uplift and a documented 7x ROI on the discount investment. There is also an algorithm ranking benefit: SnS enrolment signals recurring demand to Amazon's ranking system, driving documented position jumps of 14+ places within ten days of enrolment at even a 2% subscription rate.

Alternatively, feel free to contact us via email at info@rosettabrands.com

Amazon Business is Amazon's dedicated B2B marketplace where registered business buyers purchase in bulk with business-specific pricing, VAT invoicing and quantity discounts. UK consumer is growing at 22.5% annually on Amazon Business - with 60% of Business buyers conducting over 25% of all their purchases on the platform. Rosetta Brands enables business pricing tiers, bulk quantity discounts, business-only pricing, automated VAT invoices, and product credentials (sustainability certs, quality marks) that are only visible to business buyers.

Rosetta Brands submits, manages and optimises all deal types on behalf of brands: Lightning Deals (minimum 15% discount, £100-500 fee), Best Deals (longer duration, higher visibility), Deal of the Day, and Prime Day promotions. Prime Day 2024 generated $14.2 billion in sales and 300 million items over two days. Lightning Deals must be submitted 4-6 weeks before major events; Prime Day submissions require 6 weeks lead time. Rosetta Brands manages the full submission calendar, eligibility requirements, and post-deal performance analysis.

Amazon Vine sends your products to vetted trusted reviewers who post honest, verified reviews. For vendors, Vine costs £1,750 per ASIN enrolled - significantly higher than seller rates. It is only available for new ASINs with fewer than 30 existing reviews, and up to 30 units are distributed per ASIN. Rosetta Brands manages Vine enrolment timing and ASIN eligibility - ensuring the investment is only made on products where review velocity will have maximum impact on conversion and ranking.

Amazon UK grocery grew 20% year-on-year in 2024, growing nearly twice as fast as all other categories. Amazon now partners with Morrisons, Iceland, Co-op and Gopuff for fresh delivery. 1 in 3 units sold on Amazon.co.uk is now in everyday essentials. Amazon will launch perishable groceries with same-day delivery on Amazon.co.uk in 2026. Rosetta Brands manages vendor access to grocery and Fresh programmes, including Subscribe and Save optimisation for grocery replenishment cycles and cold chain preparation for the 2026 perishables launch.

Amazon Transparency is an anti-counterfeiting programme that assigns a unique serialisation code to every unit of a product. Customers scan the code to verify authenticity. Cost is £0.01-0.05 per barcode - very low overhead relative to the protection provided. There are no enrolment fees. Transparency is most valuable for premium brands, health and wellness products, baby products, and any brand that has experienced unauthorised resellers or counterfeit products on Amazon. Rosetta Brands advises on eligibility and manages enrolment.
