The only Vendor-as-a-Service in the UK

Your products list under our Top 15 vendor infrastructure, your shipments move on our Amazon Freight lanes, and your chargebacks land on our balance sheet. You get Prime badge, exclusive programme access, and enterprise terms from day one, not after eighteen months of building up an individual vendor relationship.

Small issues do not become expensive problems when you have a named Amazon contact working the category on your behalf.

The Rosetta Brands account runs 958+ consumer brand partnerships on a shared operational model: one supply-chain workflow into one Amazon fulfilment centre, one case-pack pallet standard, one advertising platform, one commercial contract. Outcome-based pricing
We buy your products. We carry the risk.

"They shield us from a lot of the chargebacks. They absorb that cost."

Commercial Director, UK consumer brand

Rosetta Brands absorbs 100% of Amazon's chargebacks, variable operational fees, and returns deductions into our balance sheet. We earn on a success-fee basis, tied to revenue growth on the account, rather than on a retainer or billable hours. There is nothing to justify monthly, no incentive mismatch between how we run the account and what your P&L needs, and no risk carried by the brand for the operational complexity of running Amazon.

Unmatched expertise
Experience working across multiple consumer categories.

"I trust them implicitly. I believe in relationship, and I believe in being loyal to people and fair."

Founder, UK health food brand

From grocery, beer, wine and spirits, to health and personal care, pet supplies, home and household - we have experience across multiple categories. That focus shows up in pallet economics, HFSS compliance, Subscribe & Save mechanics for consumables, pricing protection against own-label, and the operational detail that generalist Amazon partners routinely get wrong.
