The data we collect

  • IP address
  • Website usage information via cookies
  • Name and email address
  • Messages submitted through our contact form

Why we collect this data

  • To provide service information
  • To respond to requests or messages
  • To monitor website performance and usage

Our legal basis

The legal basis for collecting and processing your personal data is to perform functions in providing services to our clients and website visitors.

What we do with your data

Rosetta Brands Limited will not share, sell or rent your data to any third parties. Data sharing only occurs when legally required.

How long we keep your data

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law.

How we store your data

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure of your data.

Your rights

You may request:

  • A copy of your personal data
  • Information about how it is processed
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Objection to processing
  • Erasure of your data in specific circumstances
  • Restriction of processing where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sam@rosettabrands.com

Complaints may be filed with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at www.ico.org.uk or by calling 0303 123 1113.

Cookie policy

When you visit rosettabrands.co.uk, we may place small data files called cookies on your device. This section explains what cookies we use, why, and how you can control them.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. They allow a website to recognise your device on subsequent visits and remember certain information - such as your cookie preferences.

The cookies we use

We group cookies into three categories:

Essential cookies
These are required for the site to function and cannot be switched off. They include the cookie that stores your consent preferences so we don't ask you again on every visit. No personal data is collected by essential cookies.

Analytics cookies
With your permission, we use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors find and use the site. This data is aggregated and anonymous - we cannot identify you individually. Analytics cookies are only set after you accept them.

Advertising cookies
With your permission, we use advertising cookies from Google Ads and LinkedIn to measure the effectiveness of our campaigns and, where relevant, show you relevant content on other platforms. These cookies may share data with those third-party advertising partners. They are only set after you accept them.

Managing your preferences

When you first visit the site, a banner allows you to accept all cookies or set your preferences by category. You can change your choices at any time by clicking Manage Cookie Preferences.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling all cookies may affect how the site works. For more information visit aboutcookies.org or the ICO guidance on cookies.

Third-party cookies

Google Analytics and LinkedIn operate their own privacy policies which govern how they handle data collected through their cookies. We encourage you to review these if you have concerns:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • LinkedIn Privacy Policy

Changes to this policy

Policy updates apply immediately upon posting.
