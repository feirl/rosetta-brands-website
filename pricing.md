# Rosetta Brands Pricing

## Model: Success-fee only

Rosetta Brands operates on a pure success-fee model. There is no fixed monthly retainer, no upfront fee, and no minimum term fee.

## How it works

- Revenue is tied directly to the brand's Amazon revenue
- Rosetta Brands earns more only when the brand earns more
- Incentives are fully aligned from day one

## What affects the fee

Commercial terms vary by:
- **Category**: ambient grocery, drinks, health & personal care, pet, home & household, garden & outdoor
- **Volume**: number of active SKUs, current or projected monthly revenue
- **Services**: VaaS infrastructure only, or full service including growth marketing and platform access

## What is included

All partnerships include:
- Vendor Central access and Prime badging from day one
- Dedicated vendor management team
- Compliance and chargeback handling
- Order processing and logistics co-ordination
- Client portal access and reporting

Additional services (advertising, A+ content, brand store, photography, product launch) are available within the partnership.
